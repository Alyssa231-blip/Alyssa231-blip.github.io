Tile Flooring Installation

Back to work ↑

What this means

Tile looks great — but it only lasts when the base is solid and level. We treat tile as a structure-first job, not just a finish.

How we do it

  • Inspect subfloor and fix any soft spots or uneven areas
  • Level prep so tile lays flat and stays crack-free
  • Plan layout lines for clean spacing and symmetry
  • Install with durable materials and finish edges clean

Bathroom Remodeling

Back to work ↑

What this means

Bathrooms need correct framing support and water protection. We focus on long-term durability — not quick cosmetic cover-ups.

How we do it

  • Frame and reinforce walls for fixtures and plumbing lines
  • Prep surfaces for waterproofing and clean tile alignment
  • Install tile and finishes with tight, consistent spacing
  • Check details that prevent leaks and future damage

Kitchen Remodeling

Back to work ↑

What this means

Kitchens demand precision. Islands, cabinets, counters, and appliances all depend on a clean, strong layout underneath.

How we do it

  • Plan layout and support points for cabinetry and islands
  • Reinforce structure where loads are heavier
  • Prep floors for tile and ensure alignment for clean finishes
  • Focus on straight lines, stability, and a high-end final look

Bedroom Remodeling

Back to work ↑

What this means

Bedrooms should feel solid, quiet, and comfortable. Proper framing and prep keep walls straight, ceilings clean, and finishes looking right.

How we do it

  • Square up framing and correct any uneven lines
  • Prep for lighting and ceiling details
  • Support flooring with proper base and leveling
  • Finish with clean edges and professional attention to detail

Outdoor Living & Patios

Back to work ↑

What this means

Outdoor spaces must handle heat, wind, and rain. We build patio covers and extensions with real structure — not shortcuts.

How we do it

  • Frame strong supports (posts, beams, connections)
  • Prepare for lighting, fans, and ceiling finishes
  • Coordinate surfaces like pavers/stone with proper base prep
  • Build for long-term strength and clean curb appeal
